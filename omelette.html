Omelette

Description

Delicous omelette in under 10 mins! Use your favorite fillings.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl
  2. Heat pan to medium
  3. Grease pan with butter
  4. Pour a thin layer of eggs into pan
  5. Tilt pan so eggs cover entire surface
  6. Add cheese to the top of eggs
  7. Fold the egg in half so the cheese is in the middle
  8. Remove the egg from the pan and enjoy!
