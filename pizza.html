Pizza

Description

Pizza from scratch in under an hour. Much better than the ones you buy! Use your favorite toppings

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Set oven to high
  2. Flatten dough ball
  3. Spread sauce
  4. Add Cheese on Top
  5. Bake until golden (about 20mins)
  6. Remove from oven and enjoy!
