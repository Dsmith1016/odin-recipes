Soup

Description

Homemade soup in under an hour

Ingredients

  • Stock
  • Vegetable
  • Meat
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Heat Pot to medium
  2. Add meat until brown
  3. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add broth
  5. Cover pot and bring to a boil
  6. Add Rice
  7. Cook until rice is tender
  8. Enjoy!
